Oceanview Holdings is a private holding company that owns, operates, and invests in businesses with long-term potential across technology, finance, and media.

Our Philosophy

Oceanview Holdings takes a patient, hands-on approach to building and acquiring companies. We focus on businesses with strong fundamentals, scalable operations, and the ability to compound value over decades.

Rather than pursuing short-term returns, we build infrastructure, teams, and systems that create lasting competitive advantages. Our portfolio spans technology platforms, financial services, and digital media properties.

Founded and led by Joshua Kiefer, Oceanview operates from Columbus, Ohio with a lean, founder-driven model that prioritizes execution and long-term thinking.
